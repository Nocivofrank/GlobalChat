Chrome and Firefox extension

Talk with other people on the same page.

GlobalChat adds an optional public chat room to a webpage when you click the toolbar button. It is meant for adults who want a simple way to comment on articles, tools, videos, docs, and other pages without leaving the browser.

Privacy policy Community rules

What it does

Creates a room for the page you choose.

What it does not do

It does not watch your browsing history or open rooms automatically.

You stay in control

Chats open only after you choose a page and click GlobalChat. Private browsing pages, local files, and private network URLs are not supported.

Everything posted is public

Messages can be seen by other adult GlobalChat users who open the same page. Do not post passwords, private details, tokens, or anything sensitive.

Basic moderation is built in

People can block accounts and report messages. Moderators can remove public messages, suspend accounts, and keep records needed to handle abuse.
