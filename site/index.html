Browser extension

Public chats for the webpage you choose.

GlobalChat is a Chrome and Firefox extension that opens a shared conversation for a page only after you click the toolbar button. It does not automatically monitor browsing, collect browsing history, or record page visits.

Read Privacy Policy Community Rules

User controlled

Chats open only for the current page after explicit activation. Private browsing and local/private-network URLs are not supported.

Public by design

Messages are public to GlobalChat users who open the corresponding webpage. Do not post passwords, tokens, or private information.

Moderated

Users can report and block accounts. Moderators can remove messages from public chat, suspend accounts, and preserve audit records.
